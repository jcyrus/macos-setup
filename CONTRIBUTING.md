# Contributing to macOS Setup

Thank you for your interest in contributing to this project! We welcome contributions from the community to make this setup script even better, more flexible, and more robust for everyone.

## How to Contribute

### Reporting Issues
If you encounter any problems or have suggestions for improvements, please [open an issue](https://github.com/jcyrus/macos-setup/issues). Include as much detail as possible, such as your macOS version, terminal output, and steps to reproduce the issue.

### Suggesting Improvements
Have a great idea for a new app, a better configuration, or a script optimization? We'd love to hear it! Open a pull request or an issue to discuss your proposal.

### Pull Requests
1.  **Fork the repository** to your own GitHub account.
2.  **Clone your fork** to your local machine.
3.  **Create a new branch** for your feature or fix: `git checkout -b my-feature-branch`.
4.  **Make your changes**.
5.  **Test your changes** (verify the script runs correctly, check for syntax errors).
6.  **Commit your changes** with descriptive commit messages.
7.  **Push to your branch**: `git push origin my-feature-branch`.
8.  **Open a Pull Request** against the `main` branch of this repository.

## Guidelines
-   **Keep it clean:** Ensure your code is well-formatted and easy to read.
-   **Explain your changes:** Provide clear descriptions in your PR for why the change is needed.
-   **Be respectful:** Treat all contributors and users with kindness and respect.
